Cyber ethics

Written below is a table discussing various terms related to cyber ethics

Term Description
Privacy Respecting individuals' right to control their personal information online.
Security Ensuring protection of digital data and systems from unauthorized access.
Intellectual Property Acknowledging and respecting the ownership of digital content and software.
Responsibility Taking accountability for actions and behavior online, including respect for others.
Transparency Providing clear information about data usage, collection, and processing.
Digital Access Ensuring equal access to digital resources and opportunities for all users.
Avoiding Harm Minimizing the negative impact of online activities, such as cyberbullying or misinformation.
